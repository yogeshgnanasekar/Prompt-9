## EX-09 Exploration of Prompting Techniques for Video Generation


## Aim
To explore and understand various prompting techniques used for generating videos through AI models.

## Objective
To demonstrate how different prompt structures, such as simple prompts and detailed prompts, affect the quality, coherence, creativity, and style of AI-generated videos.

## Experiment
Learners must:

1.Create different types of prompts for video generation.
2.Generate videos using AI video generation tools.
3.Compare outputs generated from:
     Simple prompts
     Detailed prompts
     Structured prompts
     Creative prompts
4.Analyze differences in:
     Video quality
     Scene consistency
     Animation style
     Motion and transitions
     Creativity
5.Suggested AI Video Generation Tools:

     RunwayML
     Pika Labs
     Synthesia
    Kaiber AI
    Canva AI Video
    Luma AI
## Algorithm
Step 1: Select a Video Topic
Choose a simple scene or story idea.
Step 2: Create Basic Prompt
Write a simple text prompt for video generation.
Step 3: Generate Initial Video
Use AI video generation tool to generate output.
Step 4: Create Detailed Prompt
Add scene details, camera movement, lighting, style, and animation instructions.
Step 5: Generate Refined Video
Produce improved video using refined prompt.
Step 6: Compare Outputs
Compare simple and detailed prompt outputs.
Step 7: Document Observations
Analyze quality and effectiveness of prompts.
## Output


Prompts Used
AI-generated Videos
Screenshots of generated scenes
Comparison between simple and detailed prompts
Observations and analysis
## Result
Thus, the prompting techniques for AI video generation were successfully explored and analyzed using multiple prompt structure
